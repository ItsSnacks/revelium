Astaroth

Astaroth is a demon that can help you master many arcane arts similarly to Furcas. But in addition to that, Astaroth is a demon well versed in divination and he will answer your questions about events that happened in the past, are happening now or will happen in the future. Astaroth can help you learn more about yourself and the world around you.

how to summon

1. Light a candle and turn off all the other lights

2. Stare into the candlelight and quiet your mind

3. Recite an invocation. One that I’ve used successfully is: “Hail Astaroth! Seeker of Wisdom and Keeper of Time! Mighty Duke, I humbly invoke you from the depths of Hell into the Realm of Man. Hear the prayer of Your servant and friend! Lend me your powers at this time! Reveal yourself in the flames before me! Hail Astaroth! Hail Astaroth! Hail Astaroth!

4. Repeat for as many times as you think is necessary

5. Continue to stare into the candlelight. Begin visualizing energy emanating from the light and pouring itself into you.

6. Place your hand with a silver ring under your nose

7. Close your eyes and let the energy you’ve received visualize itself as Astaroth.

8. Once Astaroth has appeared in your mind either as a male or a female demon, explain your situation to the demon and humbly ask for the answers or powers you want and for what purpose.

9. When you’re done, end the ritual along these lines: “Thank you mighty Astaroth for lending me your powers and knowledge. I must end the ritual. But let our friendship extend beyond the limitations of time and space. Hail Astaroth!”

10. Blow out the candle and go into another room. Sit or lay down for a while and think about the whole experience.
